📞 Agent Script & Guidance

🎯 Opening Script
"Thank you for taking the time to review your closing costs with me today. I'm going to walk you through each section of your Closing Disclosure so you understand exactly what you're paying for. This is a $625,000 purchase, and your total cash to close will be $135,625. This includes your 20% down payment and all closing costs. Do you have your Closing Disclosure in front of you?"

💡 Section A: Lender Fees ($2,850)

  • Say: "These are fees charged by your lender, Wells Fargo, for processing and approving your loan."
  • Explain: Origination fee is 0.5% of your loan amount - this is the lender's administrative fee
  • Note: You chose not to pay discount points to buy down your rate
  • Highlight: Appraisal and credit report were already paid at application

💡 Section B: First American Fees ($2,475)

  • Say: "These are First American's fees for title insurance and settlement services."
  • Explain: "Title insurance protects you AND your lender from any ownership disputes or liens we might have missed."
  • Key Point: "You're getting TWO policies - one protects the lender, one protects YOU as the owner."
  • Value: "This is a one-time fee that protects you as long as you own the home."

⚠️ Section C: Prepaid Items ($4,125) - VARIABLE

  • Say: "These amounts may change slightly before closing based on your actual closing date."
  • Insurance: "Your annual homeowners insurance premium - you'll need proof of coverage before closing."
  • Prepaid Interest: "Interest from closing date until your first payment - this changes if closing date moves."
  • Property Tax: "We collect 6 months upfront for your escrow account."
🚨
CRITICAL: Wire Fraud Prevention "Before we finish, I need to discuss wire transfer safety. NEVER trust wiring instructions sent via email. Always call our office directly at the number you have on file to verify. We will NEVER change wiring instructions at the last minute. If you receive ANY email about updated wire instructions - it's fraud. Call us immediately."
⚠️
Set Expectations: Variable Costs "Items marked 'MAY VARY' include prepaid interest, property taxes, and recording fees. These can change based on your final closing date and county requirements. Your final amount could be $200-$500 different than shown here."
✅ Closing Script
"Do you have any questions about any of these costs? I want to make sure you're 100% comfortable with everything before closing day. Remember, you'll receive your final Closing Disclosure at least 3 business days before closing - review it carefully and call me if anything looks different. And please remember: verify all wire instructions by calling our office directly. Do NOT rely on email for wiring instructions."
